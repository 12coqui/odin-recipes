How to make a tasty "asado"


About Asado

A imagen could speak itself. When we, as Argentinian talked about Asado, we go deeper than just meat. For us, the smell of the woods burning, the music playing, the long chats with wine or beer, a few matches of "truco", and a countless list of things we do at those meetings make a meal, more like a ritual.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of meat for every person(1.5 if the cut have bones)
  • 10kg of wood or carbon(you will need a bit more if the meal is for more than 6-8 people)
  • Salt
  • 3 peppers
  • 6 eggs
  • 6 potatoes
  • Olive oil

Extras

  • White bread
  • Cheese
  • Ice
  • Salamin
  • Snacks
  • Wine, beers, cinar, soda
  • Speaker
  • Wooden table
  • Knife and fork
  • Lighter!!
Steps
  1. Light up half the wood or carbon
  2. Cut the peppers in halfs, and potatoes in slices of about 1cm
  3. Pour some olive oil over the peppers and potatoes and add salt
  4. Cut bread, salamin and cheese, open a few snancks and wait to got some ashes
  5. Open a bottle of wine, a can of beer, or just prepare a Cinar or Fernet
  6. Meanwhile, salt the meat, you will need toput over first the bigger pieces, and then the thinest
  7. Start by cocking the potatoes, peppers (facing the ashes with the inner part first)
  8. Once the veggies start to cock, you can add some meat, be careful of the ashes you put under, you won't want to roast it too much
  9. Flip the potatoes and pappers, and add cheese and an egg inside every half of papper.
  10. When eggs are ready, put the potatoes and peppers on a wooden table and eat it form there
  11. Last but not least, serve the meat and enjoy a tasty slice of Argentinian meat sorrounding by friends a good chating!
